CREDITS

Beyond Crisis is a Toronto PCM production, a collaboration of filmmakers working hard to bring you the story of what it takes to move from crisis to opportunity. Our intrepid leaders at Toronto PCM include:

  • Kai Reimer-WattsProducer / Director
  • Amanda HunterExecutive Producer / Assistant Director
  • Monica ResendesAssociate Producer / Marketing Director
KAI REIMER-WATTS PRODUCER / DIRECTOR
Kai is a proud multimedia artist, Master of Climate Change (MCC) graduate, and climate action advocate. An innovator / entrepreneur by nature, Kai recently co-founded a bold multimedia start-up “Better Creative” that celebrates the arts as a powerful tool for social change. Currently, he's busy adding his creative talents and strategic insight into the production of Beyond Crisis, guiding this exciting new story of change and transformation to address the growing climate crisis. Kai truly believes in the power of film to facilitate profound, meaningful shifts both in ourselves and in society, sharing the stories that matter most to all of us.
AMANDA HUNTER EXECUTIVE PRODUCER / ASSISTANT DIRECTOR
Amanda is a videographer and photographer passionate about bridging cultural gaps and inspiring others through video, photography, animation, and effective design. Along with working as an established portrait photographer, she has a certificate in Environmental Visual Communication, which merges environmental science with visual communication in order to effectively communicate science to a variety of audiences. Her passion for making the science and solutions around climate change visually interesting and exciting is her greatest motivator for pushing this project forward.
MONICA RESENDES ASSOCIATE PRODUCER / MARKETING DIRECTOR
Monica is an educator, researcher and community organizer. She is the current vice-chair of the board for Toronto350.org, a climate justice organization she helped to establish. Her past roles there include both Creative Director and Vice President. Monica is a consultant for the Ontario Principal’s Associations and is involved in a province-wide project to help build capacity around environmental inquiry and knowledge building in the classroom. As Associate Producer / Marketing Director, Monica is working closely with the lead Producer on continued story development, messaging and distribution.

#SMARTonCLIMATE

We believe that life is amazing - but are scared to see it under threat from our changing climate. Rather than only being shaped by the dangerous changes ahead of us, we now have an exciting chance to “shape the change” for the better, building an inspired new tomorrow. How can we do this?
By getting #SmartOnClimate.

Why take smart action on climate? Not only will this help address one of the biggest challenges of our time - the main reason we believe getting #SmartOnClimate just makes sense is because it presents a future that’s so much better than what’s currently being offered to us. Stay tuned for more details on how each of us can use the knowledge and tools already out there to shape our futures for the better.

OUR STORY &
THE PEOPLE'S CLIMATE MARCH

An exhilarating experience - launching us into an inspiring new future. That’s how many folks might describe the People’s Climate March, bringing together hundreds of thousands onto streets as far apart as New York and Beijing for a day of global action September 21st, 2014 - making it the largest mobilization for climate action in history.

This fight for our shared future didn’t stop on the streets, of course: each day, millions more are pushing forward to forge a better path in the face of climate change - building a greener, more resilient tomorrow. We’re happy to be part of that journey.
